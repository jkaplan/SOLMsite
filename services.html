Services

Our services are not designed for long term needs, which a state agency may provide, but for emergency situations.

These situations may be caused by death of a family member, divorce, job loss, or sudden unexpected expense the budget won't cover. We are, therefore, a ministry for immediate emergency relief.

We provide the following services:

  • Financial assistance with utility bills
  • Assistance locating food, shelter, clothing, and medical care by referals to other organizations
  • Send Bibles, inspirational books, or greeting cards for encouragement
  • We offer prayer with each client and remind them God is their source
  • Public speaking
