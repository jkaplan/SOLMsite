Who We Are

Shepherds of Love  is a non-profit ministry based in Edmond, OK providing encouragement to the poor by offering the Gospel of Christ and emergency financial assistance to people in crisis situations.

The following film reveals the tremendous need in our community and how Shepherds of Love responds in a biblical way.

Services We Provide

  • Financial assistance with utility bills
  • Assistance locating food, shelter, clothing, and medical care by referals to other organizations
  • Send Bibles, inspirational books, or greeting cards for encouragement
  • We offer prayer with each client and remind them God is their source
  • Public speaking
